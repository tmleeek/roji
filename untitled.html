Personal Information

In this Personal Data Protection Policy, “Personal Data” refers to

  1. any data, whether true or not, about an individual who can be identified from that data, or
  2. any other information to which Roji by Gryphon Tea Company has or is likely to have access, including data from our records which may be updated from time to time.

Such personal information may include your name, telephone number, mailing address, email address, network data and any other information relating to any individuals you may have provided in any forms, or through any forms of interaction with Roji Cha by Gryphon Tea Company.

Such personal data may be obtained through www.rojicha.com account registration process, or when updating account details. Personal data may also be collected when you request or enquire about a product or service, provide a product or service, complete a survey or questionnaire, enter a competition, participate in forums or when you communicate with Roji Cha by Gryphon Tea Company in person or writing.

YOUR PERSONAL DATA MAY BE COLLECTED WHEN:

Roji Cha by Gryphon Tea Company endeavours to ensure that the personal information we hold is accurate, complete and up-to-date. You can update your www.rojicha.com account information via the “Members Log In” section on our website or by writing in to us.

You will become a member of www.rojicha.com once you sign up for an account with us. Your name, email address, telephone number and mailing address is required during the registration process. To ensure maximum security, we strongly suggest that you create a unique username with a password.

This allows us to send our e-newsletters over to you. You may opt out of receiving future mailings or cancel your subscription(s) at any time.

Contests and Promotions

When you enter a contest or take part in a promotion, we may ask for your name, mailing address and email address. Should a third party sponsor the contest or promotion, that party will have access to this information. This information is required in order to notify the winners and, when applicable, mailing out the winners' prizes.

Public Forums

Please note that any information disclosed in public forums becomes public information. Exercise caution when disclosing your personal information. As public forums are open for users to interact with one another, Roji Cha by Gryphon Tea Company expects all users to respect the rights of others.

Note: Roji Cha by Gryphon Tea Company uses PayPal as a payment gateway, therefore we do not keep payment information such as Credit Card numbers in our database.

Purpose of collecting your personal data

We collect personal data in order to improve our products and services to serve you better. Thus, the purpose of the collection of personal data may include but are not limited to:

  • Provide you with products or services you have requested
  • Personalise and customise your experiences with us such as saving your shopping cart information
  • Help manage and enhance our services
  • Communicate with you
  • Provide you with ongoing information about the opportunities at Gryphon Tea Company that we believe may be of interest to you
  • Allow you to receive relevant emails and e-newsletters from Gryphon Tea Company

USE OF COOKIES AND IP ADDRESS

Roji Cha by Gryphon Tea Company also collects statistical information of a non-personal nature. Such data is collected automatically through a cookie located in your browser, which allows us to measure the number of site visits, average time spent, page views and other useful statistical information relating to the access of www.rojicha.com. This method of collection is performed by most webpages and will not allow you to be personally identified, as it contains only statistical information.

Roji Cha by Gryphon Tea Company may use the data in cookies to develop focused advertising targeted at a fixed group of consumers. In some cases where a webpage contains more than one cookie, the data gathered from these cookies would then be used to build statistical and demographical information. Such data is incapable of revealing your personal details to advertisers and any other third parties for any marketing or promotional purposes. You may however, configure your browser so that you are notified before a cookie is downloaded, or so that your browser does not accept cookies.

Cookies allow you to take advantage of some of Roji Cha by Gryphon Tea Company’s services, and we recommend that you leave them turned on. If you intend to disable your cookies, simply refer to the “Help” menu within your Internet browser. Note that by choosing to disable the cookie function on your browser, some features on the website may not function, and your options may be limited.

Roji Cha by Gryphon Tea Company also collects Internet Protocol (IP) addresses to help identify problems with our server and for the purposes of system administration. This unique identifier allows us to identify repeated site visitors, help keep track of users’ shopping cart contents and to gather demographic information to better understand the types of consumers that are interested in our site.

DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL DATA

  1. Processing, delivering, billing for, and collecting payment for purchased products (this excludes credit card information as Roji Cha by Gryphon Tea Company uses a third party service provider as www.rojicha.com’s payment gateway)
  2. Facilitating your purchase with our affiliated service providers and other third parties engaged to perform functions on its behalf. This can include payment processing, order fulfilment and shipment
  3. Collecting feedback
  4. Updating you on changes of our services or products, including new promotions

Pickup

Roji does not offer self-collections for purchases made via www.rojicha.com. All Singapore orders will be delivered via door to door courier delivery unless otherwise stated.

Tracking your order

Once your order has been processed, an automated email will be sent with the order details of your parcel. Delivery status will be sent upon dispatch of the parcel and you may trace your parcel using the tracking number provided.

Please note that for international deliveries, there are certain restrictions on tracking information details. You may be faced with a lack of detailed tracking information, knowing only general information such as parcel arrival at designated country only. Should you require further and more specific details regarding the status of your parcel, you are advised to contact your local postal company using the provided tracking number.

Use of cookies and IP address

Missed a delivery attempt from us? Don't worry, we'll make a maximum of 3 delivery attempts to deliver your tea order! If we fail to reach you at your specified shipping address, our delivery provider will leave you a message or give you a call. A re-delivery will be attempted on the next consecutive working day.

However, do note that reattempts do not apply to SingPost Registered Airmail & SingPost Local Registered Mail deliveries. Any failed deliveries from SingPost Registered Airmail will be re-sent back to country of origin after 7 working days of unclaimed parcel. Do note each destination countries’ Post Office process varies, do check your local post office for more details.
